================ INSTALLATION ================

To install Full Screen Background Images Pro, simply upload the unzipped folder called "full-screen-background" to your wp-content/plugins directory.

Next, navigate to your WordPress plugins menu anc click "Activate" on "Full Screen Background Images Pro".

You will now have a new menu item called "Fullscreen BG Image" under the Appearance menu. This is where you will add/edit full screen background images. You can also add new images directly from the post/page editor while creating / editing a post/page.

================ USAGE =======================

For your convienance, all usage documentation has been included inside WordPress.

Click on the "Help" link inside the "Fullscreen BG Image" menu.
